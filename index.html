The Story

In 2471 the FTL network collapses, stranding countless colonies. Among them is Kaela Voss, a cartographer who can still see the lost constellations in her mind. Pursued by a syndicate that wants her memory extracted, she commandeers a derelict cruiser and charts a course through forbidden space—where the laws of physics fray and forgotten gods whisper.
