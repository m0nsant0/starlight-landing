The Story

On a dying world bound to the twin stars Takbiru and Takmat, three species struggle for survival as the planet fractures under cosmic and political pressure. The Tears of Tiamat—crystalline blood drawn from the planet’s volcanic wounds—power cities, fuel wars, and awaken ancient prophecies. When a Vektor-3 cylinder vanishes inside the Fangs of Zarakh, a chain of conspiracies erupts: rebellions ignite, scientists are assassinated, and the Kal-Muzar spirals toward collapse.

Hether Eorahk, a hybrid geneticist, wagers everything by creating The Seven—engineered children capable of deciphering the planet’s fate—while Eliok Ekzra confronts the corruption rising within his own Council. In the shadows, Kendar Akdar, a Zar’Uk mathematician and architect of hidden chaos, bends every catastrophe toward a new order only he can control.

Between science, prophecy, and treachery, the fate of Tiamat unfolds in a world where saving life demands sacrificing it, and where truth bleeds deeper than the stone that guards it.
