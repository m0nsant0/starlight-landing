The Story

On a collapsing planet orbiting two unstable suns, three rival civilizations fight to survive as their world breaks apart under environmental collapse and political decay. Tiamat is powered by a rare crystalline resource—known as the Tears of Tiamat—formed deep within volcanic fractures. These crystals sustain cities and technology, but their extraction fuels oppression, war, and religious conflict.

When a highly sensitive scientific device disappears inside a forbidden volcanic region, the event triggers a chain reaction: rebellions spread, key scientists are murdered, and the planet’s governing council begins to fracture from within. What appears to be a crisis of resources quickly reveals itself as a struggle over knowledge, power, and control of the future.

At the center of the turmoil, a geneticist risks everything by creating seven engineered children, designed to solve the planet’s existential crisis at any cost. A veteran statesman attempts to hold the fragile political order together, even as corruption reaches the highest levels of authority. Behind both efforts, a brilliant mathematician manipulates disasters and ideologies alike, steering every catastrophe toward a new, tightly controlled world order.

Caught between science and prophecy, survival and morality, Tiamat becomes a world where saving civilization may require unforgivable sacrifices—and where the most dangerous truth is not written in scripture, but calculated in silence.
